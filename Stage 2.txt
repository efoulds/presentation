Stage 2
Gondra the dragon takes his little white crusty dog for a walk. As they walk the quiet neighborhood, the little white crusty dog suddenly disappears. Gondra panics and cries. In the corner of his eye he spots a 7 headed squirrel with blood coating its mouth. Gondra is enraged. The squirrel ate his dog and in agony he uses his projectile acid vomit to disintegrate the 7 headed squirrel. Gondra wins.
